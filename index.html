MATCHING CARD PICTURES

How to play

  • Enter your name
  • Choose a suit to play: Heart, club, diamond, spade
  • The board will be filled with 30 cards (15 pairs) randomlyshuffled.
  • Click two cards to flip them and find a matching pair.
  • If the cards match, they will stay flipped. if not, they will flip back.
  • You have limited time and limited lives, so think fast.
  • You can see your score. After matching your each pair of card, score will update with adding the value of your card matched.
  • you have scape card with value of zero.
  • Win the game by matching all the cards before times up.
  • If you lost you can try again by clicking on the restart.
